So, what is Dota TI 12?



The International Dota2 Championships 2023 (also called TI 12) is the concluding tournament of the current season of Dota Pro Circuit and the twelfth annual edition of The International which will take place at Seattle, Washington.

As the most important international event in the Dota2, TI is famous for its high bonuses. Every year, Valve invites teams from all regions to compete for the Aegis Shield.

This year's TI12 event will last for half a month. The group stage will start on October 12th, and the finals will be held on October 29th.

As part of the event, Valve also encourages the community to organize offline viewing parties. This is also the original intention of this website, to help players find suitable viewing spots and enjoy Dota with other Dota players.
